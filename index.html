TapeDeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

TapeDeck is a personal music library and player app. Your privacy matters, and TapeDeck is designed to keep your data on your devices.

Data Collection

TapeDeck does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No account creation required
  • No data shared with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

TapeDeck stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Music library database — track metadata (titles, artists, albums, genres), playlists, and playback state
  • Album artwork cache — cached artwork images for your library
  • Audio files — music files you import into the app
  • Audio embeddings — computed audio analysis data used for smart playlist generation

All of this data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Wireless Sync

TapeDeck supports peer-to-peer wireless sync between your Mac and iOS devices using Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework. This communication:

  • Happens directly between your devices over Bluetooth and/or local WiFi
  • Is encrypted (required by the app)
  • Does not pass through any external server
  • Only transfers music files and library data you choose to sync

Local Network Access

TapeDeck requests local network permission solely for wireless sync between your own devices. No data leaves your local network.

Plugins

TapeDeck supports plugins that may access track metadata within the app. Plugins run locally on your device and are sandboxed — they cannot access your full filesystem or transmit data externally without your knowledge.

Children's Privacy

TapeDeck does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.
